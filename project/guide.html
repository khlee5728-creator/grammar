AI Tool-Based Creative Grammar Learning Project Guide

English Grammar Course Team Project AI-Enhanced Learning

Project Process

프로젝트 진행 절차 (4단계)

1
교재 내용 숙지 및 문제 해결
Master Textbook Content
Read the assigned units thoroughly. Solve all textbook exercises. Identify key grammar rules and patterns.
2
프로젝트 기획 (5W1H 기반)
Project Planning
Plan your project using the 5W1H framework: What, Why, Who, When, Where, How. Define your creative direction.
3
AI Tool 기반 개발
AI-Based Development
Use AI tools to develop your creative content. Record all prompts and interactions. Iterate and refine your work.
4
비판적 검증 (Hallucination 체크)
Critical Verification
Verify all AI-generated content against the textbook. Fix errors and hallucinations. Document before/after corrections.

Challenge Missions

도전 과제 (택 1)

💬
Error Correction Chatbot
오류 교정 챗봇
Build an AI-powered chatbot that identifies and corrects grammar errors. It should explain WHY the error is wrong and provide the correct form with Korean explanations.
🎨
Grammar Visualization
문법 시각화 웹페이지
Create an interactive webpage that visualizes grammar rules with diagrams, timelines, color-coded examples, and animations. Make abstract grammar concepts visible and intuitive.
🎮
Grammar Quiz Game
문법 퀴즈 게임
Design a fun and engaging quiz game that tests grammar knowledge. Include multiple question types, scoring, feedback, and progression through difficulty levels.

Presentation Requirements

발표 필수 포함 항목

1
Grammar Theory Summary 교재 내용 요약 Summarize the key grammar rules from your assigned units. Include clear examples and explain each rule in both English and Korean.
2
Creative Extension 확장 및 변형 내용 Present your creative project (chatbot, visualization, or quiz game). Demonstrate how it works and explain its educational value.
3
Development Process Log 개발 프로세스 기록 (프롬프트 포함) Share the prompts you used with AI tools. Show the iterative process of how you developed and refined your project. Include screenshots or recordings.
4
Exam Questions (2–3) 시험 문제 출제 2-3개 Create 2–3 original exam questions that test the core grammar concepts from your assigned units. Provide answers and detailed explanations.

⚠️
Academic Integrity Warning / 학문적 진실성 경고

AI 생성물을 그대로 복사하여 제출하는 행위는 엄격히 금지됩니다.
Copying AI-generated content without modification and submitting it as your own work is strictly prohibited.

You must demonstrate your understanding by explaining, modifying, and critically evaluating all AI outputs. Your development process log should show how you guided the AI and corrected its mistakes.

AI 결과물을 설명하고, 수정하고, 비판적으로 평가하여 자신의 이해도를 보여주어야 합니다. 개발 프로세스 기록에는 AI를 어떻게 유도하고 실수를 교정했는지 보여야 합니다.
