Review: Conditionals (Units 36–38)

복습: 조건문

Second Conditional (unreal present/future)

If + subject + past simple, subject + would + base form

비현실적 현재/미래 상황

"If we took the bus, it would be cheaper."
Unreal present. 버스를 타지 않는다 (비현실)

Third Conditional (unreal past)

If + subject + had + p.p., subject + would have + p.p.

비현실적 과거 상황 (이미 일어난 일의 반대)

"If I'd known you were in the hospital, I would have gone to visit you."
Regret about the past. 몰랐기 때문에 방문하지 못했다 (후회)

📖 GIU Units 36-38, p.84-88

Review: Wish (Unit 39)

복습: 소망 표현

wish + past simple (present wish)

I wish + subject + past simple
"I wish I knew Paul's phone number."
I don't know his number, but I want to. 그의 번호를 모르지만 알고 싶다

wish + past perfect (past wish / regret)

I wish + subject + had + p.p.
"I wish I hadn't eaten so much."
Regret about the past (I ate too much). 과거에 대한 후회 (너무 많이 먹었다)

📖 GIU Unit 39, p.90

Review: Passive Voice (Units 40–44)

복습: 수동태

Passive Formation

Object + be + past participle (+ by agent)

수동태: 목적어 + be + 과거분사 (+ by 행위자)

Active

"Somebody built this house in 1981."

Passive

"This house was built in 1981."

Key Passive Tenses

Present: is/are + p.p.  |  Past: was/were + p.p.  |  Perfect: has been + p.p.  |  Future: will be + p.p.  |  Modal: must be + p.p.

📖 GIU Units 40-44, p.92-100

Review: Reported Speech (Units 45–50)

복습: 간접 화법

Backshift Rule

When the reporting verb is past tense, shift the tense back.

전달 동사가 과거형이면, 시제를 한 단계 과거로 바꿉니다.

present → past  |  past → past perfect  |  will → would  |  can → could
"I'm feeling sick." → Dan said he was feeling sick.
am feeling → was feeling 시제 후퇴

Reported Questions

ask + if/whether + S + V (yes/no)  |  ask + wh-word + S + V
"Are you coming?" → She asked if I was coming.

📖 GIU Units 45-50, p.102-112

Review: -ing and to... (Units 51–66)

복습: 동명사와 부정사

Key Patterns

Verb + -ing: enjoy, mind, suggest, avoid, finish, keep, practice
Verb + to...: want, need, decide, promise, hope, plan, offer
Verb + -ing or to... (same meaning): begin, start, continue
Verb + -ing or to... (different meaning): remember, forget, stop, try

동사 + -ing만: enjoy, mind, suggest, avoid, finish, keep, practice
동사 + to만: want, need, decide, promise, hope, plan
둘 다 가능 (같은 의미): begin, start, continue
둘 다 가능 (다른 의미): remember, forget, stop, try

📖 GIU Units 51-56, p.114-124

Review: More -ing and to... Patterns (1)

복습: 추가 동명사/부정사 패턴 (1)

Preposition + -ing (NEVER to + verb after prepositions)

without saying, interested in learning, good at singing

would rather + base form (NOT to)

I'd rather stay home. (NOT: I'd rather to stay)

used to + base (past habit) vs be used to + -ing (accustomed)

I used to live in Busan. (past)  |  I'm used to living alone. (now)

📖 GIU Units 57-59, p.126-130

Review: More -ing and to... Patterns (2)

복습: 추가 동명사/부정사 패턴 (2)

Verb + object + to: want/tell/ask someone to do

He told me to wait.

Perception verbs: see/hear + base (complete) / -ing (in progress)

I saw her cross / crossing the road.

📖 GIU Units 62-66, p.136-144

Common Mistakes Review (1)

빈출 오류 총정리 (1)

⚠ Top Mistakes Korean Speakers Make on Final Exams
"If I would know..." → "If I knew..."
No "would" in the if-clause of 2nd conditional 2차 조건문의 if절에 would 불가
"I'm used to get up early." → "I'm used to getting up early."
be used to + -ing (not base form) be used to + -ing (원형 아님)
"She said me that..." → "She told me that..."
say + (that) / tell + person + (that) say 뒤에 사람 직접 불가, tell + 사람
"I'd rather to stay..." → "I'd rather stay..."
would rather + base form (no "to") would rather + 원형 (to 없이)

Common Mistakes Review (2)

빈출 오류 총정리 (2)

"This house built in 1981." → "This house was built in 1981."
Passive needs "be + p.p." (don't forget "be"!) 수동태는 "be + 과거분사" (be 빠뜨리지 말기!)
"She asked where is the station." → "She asked where the station was."
Reported questions use statement word order 간접 의문문은 평서문 어순 사용
"I enjoy to read books." → "I enjoy reading books."
enjoy + -ing (not to + verb) enjoy + -ing (to + 동사 아님)
"without to say goodbye" → "without saying goodbye"
preposition + -ing (never to + verb) 전치사 + -ing (to + 동사 절대 불가)
